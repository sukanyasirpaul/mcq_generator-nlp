Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a rapidly evolving field of computer science that focuses on creating intelligent systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include problem solving, decision making, natural language understanding, speech recognition, and visual perception. Machine Learning, a major subset of Artificial Intelligence, enables computers to learn patterns from data without being explicitly programmed. Within Machine Learning, supervised learning uses labeled datasets to train predictive models, while unsupervised learning identifies hidden patterns in unlabeled data. Deep Learning, a specialized branch of Machine Learning, utilizes artificial neural networks with multiple layers to model complex representations of data.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is another important area of Artificial Intelligence that allows machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language. NLP applications include chatbots, sentiment analysis, machine translation, and automated question generation systems. Transformer-based architectures such as BERT and GPT have significantly improved the performance of NLP systems by enabling contextual understanding of words in a sentence. These models rely on large-scale datasets and advanced optimization techniques to achieve high accuracy.

In educational technology, Artificial Intelligence is increasingly used to develop intelligent tutoring systems, automated grading systems, and adaptive learning platforms. Automated MCQ generation is one such application where algorithms analyze textual content, extract key concepts, and generate objective assessment questions. This approach reduces manual effort for educators and ensures scalable content creation. As AI continues to advance, its integration into various domains such as healthcare, finance, transportation, and cybersecurity is transforming industries worldwide.
